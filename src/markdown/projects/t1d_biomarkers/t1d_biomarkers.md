## What are we trying to do?

PSMA is a project focused on developing Biomarkers for T1D through assessment of the shape and microstructure/microscopic makeup of the pancreas. The project incorporates clinical, genetic, and biological data, and has four specific aims:

1. To Organize, Optimize, and Assess Potential Biomarkers for T1D.
2. Cluster T1D Measurements into Stages.
3. Learn Longitudinal T1D Progression Cross-Correlations with Dynamic Biomarker Trends.
4. Learn an Optimal Policy to Track and Measure Efficacy of Treatment.
